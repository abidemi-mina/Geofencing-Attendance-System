    How Attendance Works
    1

    Open the session link

    Use the link or QR code shared by your lecturer. It opens this page directly.

    2

    Search your matric number

    Start typing your matric number and select yourself from the dropdown. This confirms your identity.

    3

    Your browser will ask for location permission. You must be physically inside the classroom or designated area.

    4

    Click Mark Attendance

    The system verifies your identity, live token, device, and location simultaneously before recording.
